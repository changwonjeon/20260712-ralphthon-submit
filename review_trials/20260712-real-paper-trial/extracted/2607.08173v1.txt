direction from instruct to reasoning behavior in weight space. The overthinking model θ Oα = θ M + α · τ reason amplifies reasoning
beyond training (α > 1). (b) Applied to a model trained to conceal the word “gold,” standard reasoning (α = 1.0) successfully hides
the secret, while amplified reasoning (α = 2.5) causes disclosure in the thinking trace before self-correction. At extreme amplification
(α = 4.0), outputs become incoherent. (c) Auditing success peaks at intermediate α values where models are destabilized enough to leak
but coherent enough to articulate—defining an effective auditing window before coherence collapse.
