- About Event (English)
Once the Ralph Loop starts, you cannot touch your coding agent directly. If you want to touch your laptop, you have to wear the lobster costume first.
After SF, Singapore, and Busan, this 5th edition celebrates ICML week in Seoul.
This time, the theme is Auto Research.

- There are two tracks:
Track 1: AI Scientist — Build an agent that creates a paper-style research artifact.
Track 2: Review Agent — Build an agent that reviews the papers from Track 1 in an ICML-style review format.

- The core experiment:
Can AI agents write papers, review papers, and help produce better research loops?
The papers made by AI Scientist agents will be reviewed by Review Agent agents. Judges will look at the papers, the reviews, and the agent workflows behind them.
